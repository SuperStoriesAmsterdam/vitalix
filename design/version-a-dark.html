// about vitalix

Built on one idea: coherence is intelligence.

Modern healthcare generates enormous amounts of health data — and then silos it by specialist, by appointment, by symptom. Vitalix is built on the premise that the real signal is in the relationship between data points, not in any single measurement.

We integrate wearable data, blood biomarkers, genetic predispositions, and personal history into a continuously updated model of your health. The AI learns your patterns. Human experts validate the insights. You keep full ownership of every data point.

Vitalix is built by SuperStories BV, a Dutch company operating as a public benefit entity. The long-term model is a data commons — collective intelligence that serves individual health, with no commercial exploitation of personal data.

// data & privacy

Your data.
Full stop.

"Your health data belongs to you. Not to advertisers, not to insurers, not to pharmaceutical companies. Vitalix will never sell, licence, or commercially exploit your personal data."

Hosted on European infrastructure (Hetzner, Germany). Encrypted at rest and in transit. Export or delete all your data at any time. No dark patterns. No hidden defaults. We're building toward steward-ownership (STAK) — a legal structure that permanently removes profit motive from data decisions.
